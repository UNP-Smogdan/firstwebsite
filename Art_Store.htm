Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean-Auguste Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was prepared in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Med: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 * 70 cm
Home: Musée du Louvre, Paris
